WHAT IS IT?

Iheardit is a system of active analysis of media consumption, based on the technology of sound recognition. Used in a mobile application enables an accurate measurement of analogue radio listenership.

Our goal is to allow you to measure your audience rate as easily as measuring the number of unique web users.

On the edge of two worlds

The dynamic development of new media made it possible to quickly and accurately analyze the consumption of content on the Internet, resulting from the bi-directional character of network communication. In the era of smartphones, on the borderline of mobile technology and the Internet, new possibilities arise. System that finally allows for equally reliable monitoring of any broadcasted - one directional content source.

Strength of simplicity

Our system does not require active participation of respondents. Single installation, supported by basic demographic data is that it takes. We achieve required speed and accuracy of the measurements by using digital signatures of the sound samples of the surroundings. Data in this form makes it impossible to extract any sensitive information. It only allows checking similarity to other samples, in this case to the original signal of a radio station.
